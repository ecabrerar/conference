        • July 16, 2026 JakartaOne Community Meetup - Dominican Republic

          JakartaOne Community Meetup Dominican Republic 2026 is a free event organized by the Java community of the Dominican Republic in collaboration with the Eclipse Foundation. It will take place on July 16, 2026, as part of the activities surrounding JConf Dominicana 2026. The meetup will feature high-level content on Java Enterprise and Jakarta EE, along with refreshments and surprises for attendees.
